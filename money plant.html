discriptions

Epipremnum aureum is the scientific name of this plant. The species is a popular houseplant in temperate regions. The plant has a multiple common names including golden pothos, Ceylon creeper, hunter's robe, ivy arum, money plant, silver vine, Solomon Islands ivy and taro vine. It is also called devil's vine or devil's ivy because it is almost impossible to kill and it stays green even when kept in the dark.
